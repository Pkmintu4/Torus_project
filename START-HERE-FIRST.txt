## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Keep Arduino IDE Closed** - It locks the COM port
2. **Check Device Manager** - Verify your COM port
3. **Test Serial Monitor** - Manually verify Arduino works
4. **Use Chrome** - Best browser support
5. **Read Docs First** - Saves troubleshooting time
6. **Start with Quick Start** - Fastest path to success
7. **Check Flask Logs** - Reveals backend issues
